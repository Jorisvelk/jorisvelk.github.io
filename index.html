Not an issue of the past

The plastic pollution of the sea isn't a new issue, but that shouldn't let us understate its importance. S.A.D. (Sea turtles Are Dying) is a project to raise awareness about this topic by making recycling bins for plastic, from plastic, in the image of a sea turtle. The plastic collected in the bins will be recycled to make more bins. This way, less plastic waste will end up in the ocean, choking up animals or smuggled back into our food.

The Turtle Can

The Turtle is the trash can, designed to be both a good use of the plastic that would otherwise pollute the ocean, and at the same time a reminder of the damage we can prevent by simply throwing away our trash properly.
